what's this website?

I got inspired by a beautiful song 'LAST HEAVEN' of my favourite Japanese Rock band called 'the GazettE'.
The lyrics are about kind of love, but its music video contains clips of stars, sea, and so on, and I started think if I could make a small photo gallery website of those scenes.
I used a very cool jQuery plugin called pjax, which is a combination of HTML5's pushState feature and ajax, so each page loads super fast.
If you want to watch the video I got inspired, you can do so by clicking 'source' on navigation. Although it's a Japanese song which you may not understand, it's still worth to listen.
